PROJECT-NAME: Freeze Frame V 1.2

MAIN-OBJECTIVE:
- Finish all 15 levels and collect as many coins as possible

GAME-BRIEFING:
Explore dangerous snow maveren's as a mighty little penguin! Careful, avoid enemies
and make sure to freeze any you see! Enemies range from very weak, to very strong. Freeze
slime spitballs enemy slimes shoot. Freeze water to get through paths to the end. Make sure to 
collect as many coins as you can as you go! Collect the key and run to the igloo to advance to the next level! Have Fun!

GAME-FEATURES:
    ENEMIES:
        - Snow Slime: Weakest enemy, can walk around and follows you, shoots only one bullet, if touched, player dies
        - Purple Slime: Second Strongest Enemy, can walk around and follows you, shoots two bullets at once, if touched, player dies
    PLAYER-MOVEMENT:
        - Player movement is top-down movement
    FREEZING:
        - Players are able to freeze enemy slimes for a short period of time. Slimes eventually unfreeze and after a random amount of freezes, slime decays
        - Freeze projectiles midway through the firing of enemy slimes, projectiles turn light blue
        and disapear once frozen
        - Players can also freeze water to manuever through paths to reach the igloo
